# Integrated analysis of single-cell and bulk transcriptomic data reveals altered cellular composition and predictive cell types in ectopic endometriosis

**Published:** PMC, 2025

- Used CIBERSORTx deconvolution on bulk transcriptomics + scRNA-seq reference.
- Identified 5 major cell types, 52 distinct cell subtypes.
- MUC5B+ epithelial cells and dStromal late mesenchymal cells identified as "dual drivers of fibrosis and inflammation."
- M2 macrophages increased in endometriosis.
- Random forest model based on cell proportions: AUC = 0.932 (excellent diagnostic performance).
- MUC5B+ epithelial cells = TOP predictive feature.
- dStromal-late cells: FXYD5 identified as overexpressed marker gene.
- EMT, cell migration, and inflammatory response pathways enriched.
- Most epithelial subtypes (SOX9_I, SOX9_f_II, Gla_s, Cil) DECREASED in endometriosis.
- MUC5B+, Lum, KRT subtypes INCREASED.
- eM2 macrophages elevated in stage III-IV endometriosis.

## Краткая справка (русский)

Интегрированный анализ bulk-транскриптомики и scRNA-seq с деконволюцией CIBERSORTx. Выявлено 5 основных типов клеток и 52 субтипа. MUC5B+ эпителиальные и dStromal-late мезенхимальные клетки — «двойные драйверы фиброза и воспаления». M2-макрофаги повышены. Рандомный лес (AUC = 0.932) — отличная диагностика. MUC5B+ — главный предиктор. Большинство эпителиальных субтипов (SOX9_I, SOX9_f_II, Gla_s, Cil) снижены; MUC5B+, Lum, KRT повышены. eM2 макрофаги увеличены на III–IV стадиях.
