## Key finding

91.9% женщин с эндометриозом имеют SIBO/IMO (vs 83.1% в контроле).

## Abstract

> SIBO/IMO prevalence was significantly higher among women with endometriosis, with up to 136 out of 148 who tested positive, 91.9% (95% CI 86.3-95.7%) against 123 in the control group, 83.1% [76.1-88.8%], P=0.0223. Women with endometriosis showed a significantly higher incidence of altered transit than those without (85.8 vs. 71%, P=0.0019) and an increased prevalence of constipation (67.8 vs. 44.7%, P=0.0017) and dizziness (44.8 vs. 28.7%, P=0.0245).

## Relevance

Прямое доказательство связи в цепочке: эндометриоз → SIBO → dizziness. Цифры большие, p-value значимые.
